# Micro-Capital Constraints in CrewAI

## The $100 Challenge within a Conversational Framework
Operating a $100 micro-capital portfolio requires exact mathematical precision. Conversational LLM frameworks naturally drift toward narrative rather than strict numeric enforcement.

## The Solution: Strict Custom Tools
To force CrewAI to respect the $100 boundary, agents are not allowed to "guess" available capital. 
- **`CapitalQueryTool`**: A custom tool returning a strict JSON response of available settled funds.
- **Mathematical Enforcement**: The `Judge Agent` is given explicit instructions to fail any task where the proposed trade size mathematically exceeds the value returned by the `CapitalQueryTool`.

## Evolution under Constraints
As the portfolio grows from $100 to $1,000, the `Risk Parity Agent` must autonomously evolve. It achieves this by updating its own sizing multiplier logic. When capital clears specific milestones, a self-evolution trigger fires, allowing the agent to expand its micro-tranche definitions.
