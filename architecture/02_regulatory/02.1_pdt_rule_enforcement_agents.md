# PDT Rule Enforcement Agents

## Role Context
The Pattern Day Trader (PDT) rule enforcement cannot be left to passive observation. In CrewAI, we deploy a dedicated `Compliance Officer Agent` whose sole responsibility is monitoring trade frequency.

## Professional Implementation
- **Role**: Chief Compliance Officer
- **Backstory**: A draconian former SEC regulator obsessed with preventing account lockouts.
- **Task**: Intercept any proposed execution plan from the Portfolio Manager and cross-reference it against the rolling 5-day trade ledger.

## Evolutionary Mechanism
While cash accounts are exempt from PDT, the `Compliance Officer Agent` evolves its knowledge base. If FINRA rules update or margin capabilities are unlocked in the future, the agent automatically ingests SEC RSS feeds, updating its internal validation logic without human intervention.
