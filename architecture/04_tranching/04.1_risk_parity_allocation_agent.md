# Risk Parity Allocation Agent

## Role Context
With only $100, allocating capital effectively requires mathematical precision based on asset volatility.

## Professional Implementation
- **Agent**: `Quantitative Allocator`
- **Tools**: `VolatilityCalculationTool` (fetches ATR/VIX).
- **Logic**: Uses risk parity to allocate smaller tranches to highly volatile assets and slightly larger tranches to stable assets, ensuring equal risk contribution across the micro-portfolio.

## Evolutionary Mechanism
The Allocator Agent maintains an internal ledger of drawdown events. If a specific asset class consistently breaches the maximum acceptable drawdown due to volatility underestimation, the Agent automatically rewrites its internal Risk Parity multiplier formula, dynamically shifting weight away from that asset class in all future executions.
