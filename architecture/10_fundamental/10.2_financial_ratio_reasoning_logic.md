# Financial Ratio Reasoning Logic

## Mathematical Objectivity
The Fundamental Analyst must synthesize raw data into an objective view of corporate health.

## Professional Implementation
- **Prompt Guardrails**: The agent is explicitly commanded to evaluate specific ratios (Debt-to-Equity, Free Cash Flow Yield, Current Ratio).
- **Execution Task**: `CalculateIntrinsicValueTask` requires the agent to utilize standard discounted cash flow (DCF) models or comparative multiples, outputting a clear intrinsic price target.

## Evolutionary Mechanism
If the agent consistently overvalues tech startups by heavily weighting Price-to-Sales over Free Cash Flow, resulting in trading losses, the Meta-Review Crew detects this correlation. It rewrites the agent's core reasoning logic, explicitly instructing it to apply heavier penalties to negative cash-flow companies in high interest-rate environments, effectively evolving its fundamental philosophy.

---

## 🛑 VC Challenge & Investor Critique
**The Arithmetic Hallucination:** VCs know that LLMs cannot accurately calculate Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) models. A DCF requires precise, compounding arithmetic over a 5-to-10-year projection array. An LLM attempting to multiply growth rates and discount rates across multiple periods will inevitably hallucinate the numbers, leading to wildly inaccurate "Intrinsic Price Targets."

### Critical User Journeys (CUJs)
1. **The Terminal Value Error:** The agent successfully calculates years 1-5 of the DCF but hallucinates the terminal growth rate multiplier. It outputs an intrinsic value of $5,000 for a $50 stock. The system leverages into a massive position.
2. **Context Window Math Blur:** The prompt includes 3 years of quarterly balance sheets. The LLM mixes up 2022's Q3 debt with 2023's Q1 revenue while calculating the Debt-to-Equity ratio.

## 🛠️ Refinement & Architecture Update
To make this robust:
1. **Deterministic Financial Modeling**: The `Fundamental Analyst Agent` is strictly forbidden from performing DCF math. The CrewAI Flow passes the extracted SEC data to a dedicated Python `FinancialModelingService` utilizing `numpy` and `pandas`.
2. **Qualitative Synthesis Only**: The Python service calculates the exact DCF value and outputs: `Intrinsic_Value: $120.00`. The LLM's only job is to compare this absolute value against the current market price and generate the qualitative thesis (e.g., "The DCF indicates 20% upside, but macro headwinds make the terminal growth assumptions risky").
