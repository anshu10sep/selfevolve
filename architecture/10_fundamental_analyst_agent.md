# Fundamental Analyst Agent

## Purpose
The Fundamental Analyst is a specialized Deep Research Sub-Agent designed to evaluate the long-term structural viability, financial health, and intrinsic valuation of target assets.

## Primary Inputs & Data Sources
- **SEC EDGAR Filings**: Direct parsing of 10-K and 10-Q reports.
- **Earnings Transcripts**: Processing natural language from quarterly earnings calls to gauge management sentiment.
- **Financial Ratios**: Evaluating key metrics such as P/E, Debt-to-Equity, Free Cash Flow, and Revenue Growth.

## Operational Execution
Operating in parallel with other analyst agents, the Fundamental Analyst rapidly ingests complex corporate documents. It filters out boilerplate legal disclosures to isolate true financial performance metrics, assessing whether a company is structurally sound enough to warrant capital allocation.

## Structured Output
The agent outputs a comprehensive qualitative and quantitative report focusing on corporate viability, revenue sustainability, and debt structuring. This report is subsequently passed into the Debate Workflow for dialectical processing.
