# Sentiment & News Analyst Agent

## Purpose
The Sentiment Analyst is a specialized sub-agent engineered to convert unstructured global narratives, breaking news flows, and social momentum into a quantifiable, directional sentiment score.

## Primary Inputs & Data Sources
- **Financial News Wires**: Real-time feeds from Bloomberg, Reuters, Benzinga, etc.
- **Corporate Press Releases**: Immediate digestion of PR Newswire and Business Wire.
- **Social Media Sentiment**: Scraping platforms like Reddit (e.g., r/WallStreetBets) and X (Twitter) for retail momentum and crowd psychology indicators.

## Operational Execution
Operating in parallel with fundamental and technical agents, the Sentiment Analyst uses natural language processing to identify immediate market catalysts. It evaluates whether current news is priced in, unexpectedly bullish, or carrying hidden bearish undertones. 

## Structured Output
The agent outputs an aggregated, weighted sentiment score (e.g., -1.0 to 1.0) along with a summary of identified immediate market catalysts. This ensures the trading ecosystem does not execute against overwhelming, irrational market momentum.
