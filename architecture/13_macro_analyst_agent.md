# Macro Analyst Agent

## Purpose
The Macro Analyst Agent evaluates the broader economic context to ground individual asset research within the systemic market environment, preventing the trading system from fighting overwhelming macro trends.

## Primary Inputs & Data Sources
- **Central Bank Policy**: Tracking Federal Reserve interest rate decisions and dot-plot projections.
- **Economic Indicators**: Parsing CPI (Consumer Price Index), Non-Farm Payrolls, and GDP data releases.
- **Sector Rotation**: Identifying broad capital flows moving between defensive and cyclical sectors.

## Operational Execution
Before the Judge Agent authorizes a trade, the Macro Analyst provides a systemic backdrop. If an individual asset displays bullish technicals but the overall market is in a confirmed macro-downtrend or reacting violently to a surprise rate hike, this agent will flag the systemic risk.

## Structured Output
Outputs macroeconomic viability assessments and systemic risk warnings, which are heavily weighted by the Bear Agent during the Debate Workflow to stress-test the overall trade hypothesis.
