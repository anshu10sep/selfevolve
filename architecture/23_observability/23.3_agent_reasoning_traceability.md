# Agent Reasoning Traceability

## The Audit Trail
When the AI loses $10, the human operator must be able to ask exactly *why* the trade was taken.

## Professional Implementation
- **The Journaling Agent**: A background agent whose only job is to consume the LangSmith execution trace and draft a human-readable "Trade Rationale" document for every execution.
- **Output**: "Trade 1045: Executed because Technical Analyst flagged RSI divergence, and Macro Analyst cleared systemic risk. Judge approved with 0.8 confidence."

## Evolutionary Mechanism
If the human operator reviews the Trade Rationale and identifies a logical fallacy (e.g., "You thought RSI divergence was bullish during a massive CPI miss?"), the operator replies in natural language. The Journaling Agent parses the human's critique and injects it directly into the Mem0 database, instantly evolving the Crew's cross-disciplinary reasoning for future scenarios.
